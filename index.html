What Makes Our Coffee Special

Our coffee has a rich and dense flavor, it is aromatic, full, slightly sweet and fragrant and delivers a long-lasting aftertaste.

It should be drunk almost bitter as the addition of sugar does not allow to detect its degree of acidity and body.

However, a high-quality coffee flavor will feature a well-balanced acidity, bitterness, sweetness, and mouthfeel combined with a dreamy aroma. Coffee beans will always be the central focus of a great-tasting cup of coffee
